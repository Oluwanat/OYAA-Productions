HISTORY AND PROFILE OF OLD AND YOUNG ART ACADEMY (OYAA).

Old and Young Art Academy popularly known as OYAA is a non-denominational and interdenominational art academy that was established in 1st October, 2018. Based on research and God given inspiration, it tends to be an academy that has the potential of changing people's lives and mindset about TALENT and when it comes too ART. It is an ordained and result-oriented academy which is filled with intellectuals, sharing LOVE, HAPPINESS, GREATNESS, PASSION etc. It was established to preach the gospel in a very unique and captivating way with the main target of turning God's people back to him in another glorious dimension with our God given talemnts through stage play (performances). It's an academy that accomodates people with different talents e.g music, dance, art and drama.
